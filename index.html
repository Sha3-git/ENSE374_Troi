Project Description

Providing a means for new runners to find good running shoes

Our project idea is to create a website that provides both novice and expert runners with the tools to find the appropriate type of shoes for their running activities. We have observed that there is an overwhelming amount of considerations to be made before the purchase of running shoes. The appropriate type of shoes for the user will be determined by the user's foot structure and over all balance, as well as the environment they will be running in among other things. Our goal with this endeavour is to improve the overall experience of newcomers to running while ensuring that they will be safe and healthy whilst running.

Our Strategic Focus Areas

LIFESTYLE:

To tap into the opportunity for our brand, our system is directed towards the average aspiring runner. We hope to encourage people to adopt a healthy lifestyle in their daily routine by removing the technical roadblocks. The running experience is more enjoyable and effective when you have shoes that fit your needs in style, comfort, and efficiency. Between the diverse brands, shoes terminology, add-on equipment, the content can be overwhelming. We are committed to reducing the stress and confusion that comes from picking the most effective, personalized, running equipment.

EDUCATION:

With the launch of our shoes recommendation system, we aspire to provide simplified explanations of all things running related. It seeks to break down the intimidating roadblock to running and provide accessible knowledge and sources. To ensure long-term results for our clients, we hope to excite and empower them by creating personalized recommendations. We also aspire to connect them to professionals and resources in the field for future reference.
